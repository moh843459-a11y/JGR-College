About The Institution

Founded in 2014 in Blackburn by the esteemed Mufti Muhammad Nizamuddin Misbahi, JGR College (جامعة غوثية رضوية) stands as a beacon of classical Islamic learning in the United Kingdom.

Our comprehensive curriculum follows the rigorous and time-tested Dars e Nizami syllabus, the globally recognized golden standard of traditional Islamic pedagogy native to the Indian Subcontinent.

Today, our distinguished alumni serve prominently as pioneering young scholars, dedicated Imams, and inspiring educators across a multitude of madāris and Islamic institutions nationwide.

Empowering Communities

We bridge classical scholastic depth with contemporary community needs, ensuring our graduates are completely equipped to counsel, guide, and lead modern societies dynamically.
