Institutional Profile

Established structural operation in 2014 inside Blackburn, United Kingdom, by the prominent theologian Mufti Muhammad Nizamuddin Misbahi, JGR College (جامعة غوثية رضوية) addresses the critical need for elite traditional Islamic higher education.

Our academic program fully carries out the rigorous framework of the authentic Dars e Nizami system—the historically vetted pedagogical model utilized across prominent training centers of the Indian Subcontinent.

Through systematic academic instruction, our graduated alumni are now functioning as leading young scholars, stewarding educational branches of major madāris, and comprehensively leading communities as dedicated Imams and teachers.

Empowering Communities

We blend classical text comprehension with essential modern leadership principles, turning out dynamic community assets tailored to guide society effectively.

The Directorate

Mufti Muhammad Nizamuddin Misbahi

Founder & Principal Headteacher

Our Principal Headteacher is a distinguished graduate of the highly prominent Islamic University, Al Jāmiah Tul Ashrafiyyah in Mubarakpur, UP, India. Possessing a stellar academic teaching tenure running across nearly 25 operational years, he stands as a recognized global authority across Fiqh (Jurisprudence), Tafsir (Exegesis), Hadith (Prophetic Traditions), and secondary rational sciences.

As a visionary structural archivist, Mufti Muhammad Nizamuddin Misbahi has contributed deeply to global Islamic scholarship by critically editing, annotating, and publishing historical legal treatises (*Tahqiq*) that were originally composed hundreds if not a thousand years ago.

A prolific speaker, he delivers complex jurisprudential data and public discourses fluently across multiple primary languages, including Urdu, Arabic, and English, bridging cultural divides across generations.
